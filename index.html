Frequently Asked Questions

Is my therapy session confidential?

Yes, all our sessions are completely confidential. We prioritize your privacy and data security.

Can I choose my therapist?

While we match you with a therapist based on your needs, you can request a change at any time if you feel it's necessary.

How do I cancel a session?

You can cancel a session up to 24 hours in advance through your account. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a fee.
